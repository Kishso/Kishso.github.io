Quick Taste

General Review

Noodles and Company in Nordeast does a good job at what they do. They are the limbo of franchise restaurants. What I mean by that is that they are technically a franchise, but it doesn't feel like a typical franchise restaurant. Their menu is just a variety list of comfort foods, so there always be something for every person. The average price for a dish ranges from $6 - $10, which is a decent price considering all the prep work you're paying to not do yourself. The atmosphere is very family friendly, and sunlight floods into the store so it always feels warm and cozy. The company rewards both their customers and employees who show loyalty. There are always pushing their rewards program, trying to promote regular customers. While also attempting to provide oppurtunities for their employees, typically the longer they have worked there, the better. It's a restaurant that will bring a unique experience not only with good food but with good values too.

Signature Dishes

Penne Rosa

Their most popular dish with a slight kick to it. Honestly can't go wrong with it. It'got all the comfort you need in one dish.

Wisconsin Man n' Cheese

Can't go wrong with a classic. Probably the safest bet for those who lack adventure

Pad Thai

First few bites are amazing. Probably one of the most filling dishes without protein.

Zucchini Truffle Mac

Delicious as much as it smells like acetone. Just don't snort the truffle powder.

Pesto Cavatappi

If you like pesto, you will love this dish. If you don't, you will still like it, just a little less.

Rigatoni Fresca

Very flavorful, and easy to please. Do not let its plain appearance fool you

Spicy Korean Beef noodles

If you like a kick, this is the one for you. Who knew ramen noodles didn't have to swim in broth.

Chicken Veracruz Salad

If you're gonna order a salad, just order this one. It's refreshing as it is flavorful.
