The Location

The Store Itself

The Store sits on the corner of Hennepin and University. The store is also an intersection for alot of bus routes. Some of which are the 6, 2, 10, and 61. The surrounding area is just the right amount of traffic, it's not super busy but it isn't a ghost town. The surrounding neighborhoods compose of alot of apartments with a younger crowd. It's not far from the Mississipii river and the area is quite green with plants and fauna. The store also isn't far from the historic district, so there is plenty oppurtunity for a walk after lunch.

The inside of the has a big lobby that stretches along the long storefront of windows. Thus, alot of sunlight pours into the store during the morning and makes the yellow walls shine. It creates a more warm, cozy atmosphere. Although during the night, the red carpet and green trim pops out more and creates a nice dinner atmosphere. There is always a mixture of old to semi-newish pop music playing just quiet enough to ignore it, but loud enough to fill in any unwanted silence.

The People

People can always make or break the dining experience. The customer base consists alot of the cosmetology students who attend Avedia right around the corner, alot of the people who live in the nearby apartments, and a staple of any wholesome restaurant, families. The employees themselves are a mix of people ranging from highschoolers to mid 30's and from multiple diffrent cultural backgrounds. Despite all their diffrences though, they are all able to bond on the same maturtiy level regardless. People there are energetic as they are easy-going.
